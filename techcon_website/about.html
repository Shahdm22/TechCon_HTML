Our History

TechCon began in 2010 as a small meetup of passionate developers and has grown into one of the world’s leading technology conferences. Over the years, we've hosted over 10,000 attendees and featured speakers from top tech companies.

Our Mission

TechCon aims to inspire innovation, promote collaboration, and shape the future of technology. We’re dedicated to bringing together minds from around the globe to exchange ideas and build lasting connections.
